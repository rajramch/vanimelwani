Welcome

If you've been feeling stuck, anxious about where life is headed, caught in overthinking, or like you're constantly falling short no matter how much you try — you're not alone. Therapy and self-discovery are about creating practical ways to feel more grounded and in control. Let’s work together to understand your mind and move forward.

How I Can Help

  • Understand your thoughts, emotions, and behaviors
  • Manage anxiety, self-doubt, and procrastination
  • Navigate relationships and boundaries
  • Break old patterns to create new ones
  • Build healthier habits and self-compassion
  • Move from just coping to thriving

My approach combines evidence-based techniques with warmth and honesty, using CBT, REBT, and ACT for real change.

About Me

I'm a psychologist with a Masters in Clinical Psychology from Mumbai University. My skills are certified by Morphic Minds (Level 1 & 2 CBT and REBT), with supervision and mentorship from Dr. Shishir Palsapure and Ms. Asira Chirmuley.

I work with adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, depression, ADHD, OCD, professional stress, burnout, and relationship challenges. I’ve conducted workshops for:

  • The Cathedral and John Connon School, Mumbai
  • Ramdeobaba University, Nagpur
  • Sophia Women’s College, Mumbai
  • Tirpude Institute of Management Education (Visiting faculty)
  • Dr. Hedgewar Hospital, Amravati
